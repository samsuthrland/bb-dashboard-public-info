Privacy Policy

Boston Barbell Dashboard · Last updated: May 14, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Boston Barbell internal analytics dashboard ("the App"), a private tool used solely by the Boston Barbell account owner to view performance data from connected social media accounts.

Data We Access

The App connects to TikTok via the TikTok API to read the following data belonging to the authenticated account owner only:

  • Basic profile information (display name, username, avatar)
  • Account statistics (follower count, video count)
  • Video performance data (views, likes, comments, shares)

How We Use Data

All data is used exclusively to display analytics within the private dashboard. Data is stored locally on the account owner's machine and is never shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Data is cached locally for up to 6 hours and may be cleared at any time by the user. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond what is required to authenticate with TikTok's API.

Single User

This application is a private, single-user tool. Only the Boston Barbell account owner can authenticate and use the dashboard. It is not a public-facing application.

Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, contact: info@bostonbarbellofficial.com
